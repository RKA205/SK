The Infrastructure

The School infrastructure has been designed so as to incorporate all the facility necessary for imparting holistic education.

Library
The Library has rich collection of Books on various subjects and from across the world. It includes Fables, Short Stories, Novels, Mythology, Encyclopedias, Dictionary, Maps & Charts, reference Books, Magazines and News Paper etc. It also has provision for Audio-Visual with rich collection of Cd's/DVD's. In the series of our effort to provide world class education we have smart class in our school powered by Educomp.
Art, Craft & Other Facilities

The School has facility to train students in various creative activities such as Art & Craft, Dance, Drama & Music etc. It also provides necessary training for students interested in Marshal Art, Yoga etc.

Science Lab

The Composite Science Laboratory is fully equipped to fulfill the requirement of observation, demonstration and experimentation in Physics, Chemistry Biology and Math’s Lab Separately dedicated for each subject up to Senior Secondary School Level.

Computer Lab

The Computer Lab has latest computer together with internet-Wi-Fi Connectivity.
